Services

Boarding, Training, Day Care.

Training

Do you want to be able to communicate with your four-legged companion?

In just two weeks, your dog can learn the basic commands and we will teach you how to work with your dog and improve your relationship. Our method is positive reinforcement using a clicker, a device that marks the exact moment when the animal does something we want them to do.

Boarding

Going on vacation or to a place where it is difficult to take your pet?

We have the perfect solution for you and your companion. Our boarding consists of a kennel-free day camp. If your dog is in training or simply boarding, they all get to socialize and have fun their entire stay. We also have special accommodations for cats.

Day Care

It is very important that your pet has a healthy quality of living.

If you spend a lot of time working during the day and your pet is alone at home; we welcome him during the day to join our team to socialize and stimulate his mind.

About

Tails of Discipline is a dog training school that focuses on quality time spent on both the education and socialization of dogs through positive reinforcement practices. Established in 2019 in Isabela, Puerto Rico.
